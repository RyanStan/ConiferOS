qemu-system-i386 -drive file=bin/disk.img,index=0,media=disk,format=raw

How to run with gdb:
	make sure to use tmux and split panes
	in one pane run: 'qemu-system-i386 -s -S -drive file=bin/disk.img,index=0,media=disk,format=raw'

	Then in gdb: 
		'add-symbol-file build/kernel.o 0x100000'
		'target remote localhost:1234'

Helpful gdb commands:
	'layout asm'
	'info registers'
