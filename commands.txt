nasm -f bin ./boot.asm -o boot.bin

qemu-system-x86_64 -hda ./boot.bin

How to run with gdb:
	make sure to use tmux and split panes
	in one pane run: 'qemu-system-x86_64 -s -S -hda ./boot.bin'

	in the other pane run: 'gdb'.  Then in gdb: 'target remote localhost:1234'

Helpful gdb commands:
	'layout asm'
	'info registers'
